Block Puzzle Pro does not use accounts, logins, or sign-in of any kind. This means we do not collect your name, email address, or any information that directly identifies you. Because there is no account tied to your identity, data deletion works a little differently than in apps that require sign-in — see the steps below for your region.

Data we collect: anonymous crash reports, approximate location (city/region level, for analytics only), device/usage information, and an advertising identifier used to serve ads. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or precise GPS location.

How to Delete or Reset Your Data

1. Reset your Advertising ID (all users, all regions)

This is the primary identifier used for ad personalization. Resetting it breaks the link to any previously collected ad data:

  1. Open your device's Settings
  2. Go to Google → Ads (or Privacy → Ads, depending on your Android version)
  3. Tap Delete advertising ID (or Reset advertising ID on older versions)

2. Manage ad consent preferences (in-app)

Open Block Puzzle Pro → Settings → Consent Settings to review or change your ad personalization consent at any time.

3. Uninstall the app

Uninstalling removes all locally stored data, including game progress, from your device immediately.

4. Contact us directly

For any request not covered above, email us at [your-support-email@example.com]. We respond to all data-related requests within 30 days.

Region-Specific Rights

🇪🇺 EU / EEA / UK Users (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, restrict, or erase your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Since we hold no directly identifying data, exercising these rights is done via the Advertising ID reset and Consent Settings steps above. You may also contact us at the email above for any additional request, and you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

🇺🇸 California Users (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to opt out of the "sale or sharing" of personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. You can exercise your opt-out right using the Consent Settings in-app, or by resetting your Advertising ID as described above. We do not sell personal information for monetary payment.

🌍 All Other Regions (including India, Brazil, and elsewhere)

We apply the same practices globally, regardless of local legal requirements. Any user, anywhere, can use the steps above — Advertising ID reset, in-app Consent Settings, or emailing us directly — to manage or request deletion of their data.
